AI Detective

DEVELOPMENT

Upload a video to the cloud, and the AI Detective will analyze your video for deception.

Algorithm Summary

  • Stage 1: Face tracking.
  • Stage 2: Speech to text.
  • Stage 3: Extract movement and microexpressions.
  • Stage 4: Extract vocal trends.
  • Stage 5: Pass all data through a multimodal machine learning model.
  • Stage 6: Display results to you.

Some tips on submitting videos with accurate results:

  • Isolate the query statement (i.e. don't submit a monologue).
  • Keep it short. Ideal videos are less than a minute long.
  • Ensure the subjects face is in the frame the whole video facing the camera.
  • Trim the video as short as possible.
  • Record clear audio.
  • The higher the stakes of the potential liar the better.
